SR 520 Bridge Toll Rates

Tolls are collected in both directions of the bridge. 

SR 520 toll rates for two-axle vehicles (includes motorcycles) as of July 1, 2017

  • The Good To Go! weekday and weekend toll rates increase 5 percent (equates to peak period tolls increasing 20 cents).
  • Nighttime tolling (11 p.m. – 5 a.m.) begins at a flat rate of $1.25 per crossing for those with a Good To Go! pass.

Motorcycles typically have two axles and pay the same toll as larger two axle vehicles. Vehicles with more than two axles will pay a higher pro-rated toll rate (pdf 141 kb).

Toll rate setting and process

The Washington State Transportation Commission (WSTC) works with WSDOT and the public to establish toll rates for all highways and bridges in Washington State. The WSTC reviews traffic and revenue each year to determine whether new toll rates are needed to cover operational costs and debt payments. This review process starts in the fall and, if new rates are adopted, they are in effect the following July. Learn more about toll rate setting process .
